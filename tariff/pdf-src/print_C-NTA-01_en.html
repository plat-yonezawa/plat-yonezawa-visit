II · The Experience

In Yonezawa, seasons are not four, but twenty-four. While the modern world rushes by, the Nitta family has spent generations capturing the subtle breath of nature. This exclusive session opens the family’s private residence, hosted by Chairman Nitta and his family. Witness the alchemy of natural dyeing — silk dyed with Japan red (safflower) — and the craftsmanship behind colours that have defined Yonezawa for generations. Through a tea ritual, dialogue, and a hands-on dyeing experience, you touch the essence of careful living.
